Who we are

Your Trusted Financial Partner

Hexagon Finance was founded to make borrowing simple, transparent, and fair. We work with salary earners, entrepreneurs, and small businesses across Uganda who need fast, honest access to credit — without confusing terms or hidden charges.

Every product we offer is built around three principles: clear pricing, realistic repayment plans, and decisions you can act on the same day you apply.
